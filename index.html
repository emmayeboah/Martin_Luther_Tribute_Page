Here's a time line of Dr.Martin Luther King Jr. life:

  • 1929 - Born on January 15
  • 1955 - Dr.King led Montgomery bus boycott
  • 1955 - Dr.King became the first President of the Southern Christian Leadership Conference(S.C.L.S)
  • 1963 - He helped organize the nonviolent protest in Birmingham, Alabama.
  • 1963 - Dr. King helped organize the March on Washington, where He delivered his famous "I Have A Dream" speech on the steps of the Lincoln Memorial.
  • 1964 - Won the Nobel Prize for Compating Racial Inequality through nonviolent resistance.
  • 1965 - He helped organize the Selma to Montgomery marches.
  • 1965 - On April 4, King was assasinated.
